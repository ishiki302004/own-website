My Top 5 Favorite Singers

1. Blackpink



Blackpink is a South Korean girl group founded by YG Entertainment, consisting of Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. The trio made their debut in August 2016 with their single album Square One, which included the number-one singles "Whistle" and "Boombayah" on South Korea's Gaon Digital list and the US Billboard World Digital Song Sales list, respectively.

2. Taylor



Taylor Swift is a pop and country music singer-songwriter from the United States. Five of her songs have charted on the Billboard Hot 100, including "Shake It Off" (2014), "Blank Space" (2014), and "Look What You Made Me Do" (2017). She won several honors for her songs.

3. Lorde



Ella Marija Lani Yelich-O'Connor, better known as Lorde, is a New Zealand singer-songwriter. Her stage name was inspired by aristocracy, and she is noted for her unique musical styles and introspective songwriting. Lorde displayed an interest in playing at local venues when she was in her early twenties.

4. Arian Grande



Ariana Grande-Butera is a singer, songwriter, and actor from the United States. Her four-octave vocal range and unique use of the whistle register have earned her great accolades from music reviewers and the media. Her personal life and songs have received a lot of media interest.

5. Billie Eilish



Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O'Connell is a singer and songwriter from the United States. Her debut hit "Ocean Eyes" was written and produced by her brother Finneas O'Connell, with whom she collaborates on music and live shows.
